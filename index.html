RDT-1B:
a Diffusion Transformer Trained on Multi-Robot Data for Bimanual Manipulation

Author 11
1Tsinghua University
Paper arXiv Code Data

头图视频

To resolve the challenges of data scarcity and multi-modality in bimanual manipulation, we present Robotics Diffusion Transformer (RDT) with 1.2B parameters, which is the largest diffusion-based pre-trained model for robot control, pre-trained on the largest multi-robot dataset collection.
